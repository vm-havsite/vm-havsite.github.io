Privacy Policy for Havsite



Effective Date: 15 AUGUST 2025 (IST)



Thank you for using Havsite. We take your privacy seriously and want to be transparent about the data we collect, how we use it, and the control you have over it. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following user information:

  • Username – used to identify users uniquely, support anonymity, and enable peer-to-peer (P2P) chat functionality.
  • Email Address – used for account-related communications and authentication.
  • Password – securely stored via Firebase Authentication.
  • Chat History & Attachments – messages and file attachments exchanged during chat sessions.
  • First-Party Cookies – used for theme personalization and session management.
  • User Metadata – includes site visit counts, username, and usage activity.

2. How We Collect Information

All user data is collected through:

  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore – for account data, chat messages, and cookie data.
  • Appwrite Storage – for storing file attachments shared in messages.
  • Google Analytics & Search Console – for monitoring site usage, activity, and SEO performance.

3. Why We Collect Your Data

We collect and use your data for the following purposes:

  • To maintain user accounts and authenticate access.
  • To support a personalized, cookie-based user experience (like light/dark themes).
  • To provide and maintain real-time chat functionality.
  • To ensure platform performance and monitor usage trends.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only as follows:

  • Google Analytics & Search Console –receives metadata like usernames and visit activity.
  • Firebase – stores all account and chat-related data securely.
  • Appwrite – stores file attachments; access is restricted to the sender and receiver.
All other personal data remains exclusively within our Firebase environment.

5. Cookies

We use first-party cookies to:

  • Remember your theme preference (e.g., dark or light mode).
  • Maintain your sign-in status between sessions.
You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings

6. Data Security

We apply modern security measures to protect your data. Authentication is handled by Firebase, and files are stored with restricted access in Appwrite.

Note: While we aim to keep your data secure, this is a developer-maintained app still in active development. Your understanding is appreciated as we iterate and improve.

7. Children’s Privacy

Havsite is not formally restricted to any age group. However, we advise users under the age of 10 to refrain from using the platform without parental guidance.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Request deletion or modification.
  • Opt out of data collection (except where necessary for app functionality).
To exercise your rights, contact us at: vm002248@gmail.com

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. All changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of Havsite after such updates constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

10. Contact Us

For questions, requests, or concerns regarding this policy, contact: https://vm-havsite.github.io/help
